Cracking: An Exploration of Generative Design


This book documents my exploration of natural algorithms, primarily cracking, as form generator. Haiku is an algorithmic poetry form that is commonly used to reflect on nature, making it an apt form of communication of the subject.



The exploration includes hand drawing, Adobe Illustrator drawing, physical prototypes, Rhino3D models, Grasshopper algorithms, and Processing algorithm outputs.
