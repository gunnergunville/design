About

Jason Matthew Gunville

First and foremost I am a big picture thinker. You can't understand the forest without understanding the trees, the terroir, the climate, and all the ecosystem's stakeholders. The design process is a tool with which any system can be understood, and leveraged towards improvement.

I am a creative problem solver and solution generator. A mediator of variables and balancer of tensions. These skills are borne from a desire to do better, to improve, and a mind open to diverse perspectives.

I am an astute generalist. I am comfortable with a wide variety of visualization tools and techniques. I can draw and 3D model, make and build, design and discuss. I even coded this website. If I don't know how to do something, I will learn.

I am a people person. I've learned a lot about people through my experience in the service industry. One of the most important aspects of delivering exceptional service is intuiting your clients needs. I have great intuition. Beyond service I've also been a teacher of children. You can learn a lot about people by observing kids. People are at the center of every design challenge. Understanding them isn't difficult, in my experience people want to be understood. It can be as simple as asking, and listening intently to their answers.
